## 1. What is CLV (Closing Line Value)?

In professional sports markets, the "Closing Line" (the price at tip-off) is considered the most efficient and accurate price possible. 

The only consistent way to be a profitable market operator is to beat the closing line. If you bet a favorite at **-3** and they close at **-5**, you have generated **Positive CLV**. 

**Stratum's primary mission is to identify moves that beat the closing line before they happen.**

## 2. Automated Signal Auditing

Every signal in the Stratum database undergoes a lifecycle of verification:

1.  **Detection**: A signal (e.g., `STEAM`) is triggered by 5+ books moving simultaneously.
2.  **Relay**: The signal is dispatched via Webhooks/Discord to our partners.
3.  **Settlement**: 60 minutes after game-start, our engine automatically fetches the final "Close" from the market leaders (Pinnacle, Circa, etc.).
4.  **Audit**: We compare our "Entry Price" (when the signal was sent) to the "Closing Price."
    *   **Success**: The market continued in the direction of our signal.
    *   **Failure**: The market reversed or stayed flat.
